About Me

My Story

Growing up, I was full of ideas but had no outlet to bring them to life. That changed in 2020 when I enrolled in a computer school named Aptech Computer Education, where I was introduced to the basics of programming. From the very first day, I was interested and inspired by coding. I was eager to learn more, so I began taking online courses on Udemy and diving deeper into the world of code.

Two years in, I’ve built a range of unique and exciting projects. But more than the successes, it's the countless errors I’ve encountered that have sharpened my problem-solving skills and shaped me into the programmer I am today.

Today, I’m an efficient, dedicated developer who values every chance to code and collaborate. Let’s work together to turn your ideas into reality.
